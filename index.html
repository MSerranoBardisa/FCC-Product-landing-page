CURRICULUM

Follow a tailored programme to land your dream job

  • Week #1 - Meet your advisor

    The first step of the programme is to match you with your industry coach. He or she will come from the background and firms you are targeting. For instance, if you want to become a management consultant, we'll match with an alumni from McKinsey. If you want to land a software engineering job, you'll get paired with a former developer from Google. Etc.

  • Week #2 - Improve your resume and online presence

    Your coach and the SchoolFour team will then work with you to make significant improvements to your resume and social profiles (e.g. LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.) This step makes a huge difference. It will ensure a step-change in the number of applications you make that result into an interview.

  • Week #3 - Build an application pipeline

    We believe job application is about targeting the right opportunity. You will spend time with us to determine which jobs you should apply for and to maximise the chances of success of your applications by networking and tailoring your resume and cover letter. Whevener possible, your coach and the S4 team will make introductions to our network.

  • Week #4 to 7 - Become an interview pro

    Once your application strategy will be fine-tuned the interview requests will start coming in! You'll work with us to build an interview preparation programme. We've got training materials for all types of interviews: coding interviews, case interviews, finance interviews, etc. You're in good hands!

  • Week #8 - Negotiate your salary

    Most candidates we work with start getting offers after 8 weeks or so of working with us. At that point, our team will help you review the offer you are made and negotiate. This can significantly improve the compensation package you achieve and often ends up more than covering the costs of the S4 programme itself.
